Babe Ruth

"Baseball is the greatest game in the world and deserves the best you can give it."

Babe Ruth at the plate

Here's a time line of Babe Ruth's life in baseball:

  • 1895- Babe Ruth was born in Baltimore, Maryland.
  • 1902- Babe Ruth attended St. Mary's industrial school for boys, where he was first introduced to the game of baseball.
  • 1914- Ruth signs his first contract with minor league baltimore Orioles.
  • 1918The Boston Red Sox wins the world series!
  • 1919The New York Yankees gives Ruth an offer so large he couldn't refuse.
  • 1923Babe Ruth win MVP (Most valuable player).
  • 1926Yankees lose the world series.
  • 1928Yankees came back and swept the St. Louis Cardinals in four games winning the World Series.
  • 1929Ruth marries Claire Merritt Hodgson
  • 1932Babe Ruths called shot, winning home run hit by ruth himself.
  • 1935Ruth is sold to the Boston Braves
  • 1936Ruth is elected into Baseball Hall of Fame.
  • 1942"The Pride Of The Yankees" Babe Ruth's biographical film is released.
  • 1946Experiencing severe pain over left eye, Babe Ruth is diagnosed with a Malignant Tumor in his neck
  • 1948Babe Ruth dies.

“I'm going over the valley.” - Babe Ruth's famous last words.
